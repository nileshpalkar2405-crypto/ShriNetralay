About Shri Netralay

Committed to providing exceptional eye care with compassion and expertise

Our Founder

Vivek Palkatr

Vivek Palkar is an experienced professional with an extensive background in spectacle manufacturing, optical services,with BSc in Optometry and modern eye-care solutions.

With deep knowledge, years of hands-on experience, and a passion for helping people regain clear vision, our founder ensures that every patient receives the best care, honest guidance, and high-quality treatment.

Their dedication towards providing affordable and accessible eye-care services has helped countless patients achieve better vision and confidence.

Our Mission

At Shri Netralay, our mission is to provide comprehensive, compassionate, and cutting-edge eye care services that improve and preserve the vision of our community. We strive to make quality eye care accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or financial situation.
